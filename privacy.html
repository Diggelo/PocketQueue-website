Privacy Policy

Pocket Queue is designed to be simple and private. The app does not require an account and does not track you.

What we collect

  • No personal data. We do not collect names, emails, or identifiers.
  • No tracking. We do not use advertising identifiers or tracking SDKs.
  • No analytics. Pocket Queue does not include analytics in v1.0.

What you store

  • Your titles, notes, ratings, status, and sources are stored locally on your device in v1.0.
  • If iCloud sync is added in the future, it will be opt-in.

Third-party services

  • App Store / StoreKit. If you choose to tip via the in-app Tip Jar, Apple processes the payment.
  • Pocket Queue does not receive your payment details.
