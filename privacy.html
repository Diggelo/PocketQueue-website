Privacy Policy

Last updated:

The Short Version

Pocket Queue is built with a "Privacy First" philosophy. We don't want your data, so we don't collect it.

What we collect

  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • No Tracking: There are no hidden SDKs or analytics tools watching what you do.
  • No Accounts: You don't need to log in to use the app.

Where your data lives

Everything you add to Pocket Queue stays on your iPhone. In version 1.0, there is no cloud sync, meaning your data never leaves your device unless you back up your phone to iCloud.

Contact

If you have any questions, feel free to reach out via the Contact Form.
