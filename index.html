Features

  • Quick Add: Catch a title instantly and keep moving.
  • Search + filters: Find anything by title, notes, or source.
  • Status tracking: Backlog, in progress, completed — with fast swipe actions.
  • Continue & Recently added: Jump back in, or see what you added last across categories.
  • Random pick: One tap to break decision paralysis.
  • Local & private: No account. No tracking. Your list stays on your device.

Privacy

  • No tracking: Pocket Queue does not track you, sell data, or use advertising identifiers.
  • Local storage: Your items are stored locally on your device in v1.0.
